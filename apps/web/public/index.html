About CredX

Meet J. Malloy, CEO and creator of CredX.

J. Malloy built CredX to be more than a credit repair brand. The vision is a structured credit and financial positioning platform that helps people repair, rebuild, and rise with more clarity, stronger systems, and practical next steps.
