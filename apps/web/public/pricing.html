How & when you're billed

Free credit analysis — $0

The credit analysis happens before any payment is requested. There is no charge and no card required to receive it.

5-Day Masterclass (education) — billed at signup

The Masterclass is an educational digital product, charged once at signup with immediate access. Refunds follow the Masterclass Terms (7-day window, subject to content accessed).

Done-with-you / White-glove (support services) — billed after work is performed

Paid support plans are optional and begin only if you choose to continue after your free analysis. Consistent with the Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA), you are not charged at signup:

  • Nothing due today. You sign a written Service Agreement and we begin work — no payment is collected up front.
  • First work fee is charged only after your first round of disputes has been prepared and sent.
  • Monthly fee is billed in arrears — after each month's work is performed, never in advance.
  • Cancel anytime, including your three (3) business-day right of cancellation under CROA. You are never billed for work that has not yet been performed.
  • No specific credit score, item removal, or other outcome is guaranteed. You may dispute inaccurate information yourself, for free, under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA).
