Key Questions

How this SaaS version would work

Can the program and masterclass live together?

Yes. They should be two entry paths into the same CredX ecosystem, with the platform holding profiles, education, support, and upgrade opportunities.

Can AI be the support layer?

Yes. AI can handle explanations, reminders, navigation, and education. You remain the human strategy and trust layer where needed.

Can 10 city agents use this?

Yes. Agents can be lead generators and local closers while the platform standardizes delivery and AI reduces operational bottlenecks.

Is this more defensible as SaaS?

Much more. The software is clearly central, the masterclass is a real module, and your coaching is a premium layer on top of the platform.
