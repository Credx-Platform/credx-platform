Common Errors That Hurt Your Score

Identity Errors
Accounts that don't belong to you, wrong personal information, or mixed files with a similar name.
Accounts you never opened
Duplicate Accounts
Same debt listed multiple times, often when sold to collection agencies — artificially inflating your debt burden.
Same account, different collectors
Status Errors
Accounts showing open when closed, incorrect late payments, wrong balances, or incorrect credit limits.
Late payments you made on time
Outdated Information
Negative items that should have aged off after 7 years (10 for bankruptcies). Old addresses and employers.
8+ year old collections
Balance Errors
Incorrect credit limits showing higher utilization, wrong account balances, or payments not properly credited.
Balance higher than your limit
Fraud Indicators
Accounts opened without your knowledge, inquiries you didn't authorize, or addresses where you've never lived.
Inquiries you didn't apply for
