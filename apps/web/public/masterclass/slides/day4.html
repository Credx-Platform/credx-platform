Authorized User Strategy

How It Works: When someone adds you as an authorized user on their credit card, that card's entire payment history appears on your credit report — often instantly and with no hard inquiry.
You don't need to use or possess the card to benefit.

Secured Credit Cards That Build Credit

How It Works: You provide a security deposit ($200–$500) which becomes your credit limit. Works exactly like a regular card and reports to all three bureaus.
Your deposit is refunded when you close or graduate to unsecured.
Best Practices
1
Small purchases monthly
Show activity; keep utilization under 10%
2
Pay in full every month
Never carry a balance or pay interest
3
Set up autopay
Never miss a payment under any circumstances
4
Wait for graduation
Typically 6–12 months of good payment history
